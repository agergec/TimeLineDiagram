üìä Working with Lanes

Adding & Naming Lanes

Lanes represent different components in your system‚Äîservices, users, databases, or any logical grouping. Click + Add to create a new lane, then click on its name in the sidebar to edit it.

Reordering Lanes

Use the up/down arrows that appear when hovering over a lane in the sidebar to change the order. You can also drag lanes to reorder them.

Lane Colors

Click on the colored square next to a lane name to open the color picker. All boxes in that lane will use variations of this base color.

üí° Pro Tip

Use contrasting colors for adjacent lanes to make your diagram easier to read at a glance.

üì¶ Working with Boxes

Creating Boxes

Click and drag horizontally on any lane track to create a new box. You can drag in either direction‚Äîleft-to-right or right-to-left‚Äîand the box will be created correctly. The leftmost point becomes the start time, and the drag distance determines the duration.

Editing Boxes

Click on any box to select it and open the properties panel. You can edit:

  • Label ‚Äî A name or description for the activity
  • Color ‚Äî Pick from the palette or use the color picker
  • Start Time ‚Äî When the activity begins (in milliseconds)
  • Duration ‚Äî How long the activity lasts

Resizing Boxes

Hover over the left or right edge of a box until you see the resize cursor, then drag to adjust the start time or duration.

Moving Boxes

Drag a box horizontally to change its start time. The box will stay in its lane.

üí° Pro Tip

Use the "üìç" button next to Start Time to pick the end time of another box‚Äîperfect for showing sequential activities.

üìè Measurement Tool

The measurement tool lets you precisely measure time distances between any two points on your diagram. It features smart snapping to box edges, pinning for persistent display, and export support.

How to Use

  1. Activate Hold Cmd (Mac) or Ctrl (Windows/Linux) and left-click on the diagram canvas.
  2. Drag to Measure While holding the mouse button, drag to your second point. A phosphorus green line shows the measurement with elegant |------>| markers.
  3. Read Results The info box displays duration, start time, and end time of the measured range.
  4. Pin or Close Click üìå to pin the measurement and keep it visible, or √ó to close immediately. Unpinned measurements fade after 2 seconds.

‚ú® Smart Snapping

The measurement tool automatically snaps to vertical alignment lines‚Äîthe start and end points of boxes. When your cursor is within 8 pixels of a box edge, it snaps to that exact position, making it easy to measure precise distances between events.

üìå Pin & Export

When you pin a measurement:

  • The measurement stays visible until you unpin or close it
  • The info box gets a glowing green border
  • Pinned measurements are included in PNG and SVG exports!

This is perfect for highlighting specific time intervals in your exported diagrams or presentations.

üí° Pro Tip

Use pinned measurements to annotate your exports‚Äîshow the gap between API call and response, highlight a critical time window, or compare durations across lanes.

‚ö†Ô∏è Note

Creating boxes is disabled while measuring. Release the mouse and ensure you're not holding Cmd/Ctrl to resume normal editing.
